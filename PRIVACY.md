# Privacy Policy (Draft)

Last updated: 2026-03-01

## Summary
RepairBridge processes vehicle identification numbers (VINs) and related lookup results to provide recall, complaint, and repair information. This draft policy is intended for internal review only and must be finalized before production use.

## Information we collect
- VINs submitted by users
- Lookup results returned by third-party data providers (e.g., NHTSA)
- Usage metadata (timestamp of lookup)

## How we use information
- To deliver vehicle data, recalls, and complaint summaries
- To generate reports requested by users
- To improve product reliability and performance

## Data retention
- Draft: 30 days for lookup metadata; report history saved until user deletes

## Third-party services
- NHTSA APIs (public data)
- Any additional providers explicitly configured
